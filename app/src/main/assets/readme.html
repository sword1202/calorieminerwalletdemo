How to Play

 

To play the Avatar:

 

Select Start Game, then click on Play Avatar. Random numbers using the system timestamp as seeds will determine the bit assignments.

 

To play a Human:

 

1. Make sure that they have Blinkadoo installed on their phone. You may select Send Invitation from the menu to send them an installation link via e-mail or SMS. When you select Send Invitation, you may enter a message indicating that you will be the Starter and they will be the Joiner.

 

2. The Starter is the one that decides how many rounds are played. The Starter should select Start Game and get ready before the other player selects Join Game. The Joiner must then enter the Starter’s Gmail in entry box, and then click Find Starter. After the Starter is found, the Joiner must click Join Game – Select. Then, when ready, the Joiner must click Ready to Start. The Starter and Joiner can then start the game.

 

3. A randomly generated bit, a 0 or a 1, is generated for each player. If you think that the other player was assigned the same bit as you, select SAME, otherwise select DIFFERENT. After each blink is completed, you will hear two tones. If both players made a correct selection, then you will hear two high tones. If both players selected incorrectly, then you’ll hear two low tones. If you selected correctly and they selected incorrectly, then you will hear a high tone followed by a low tone. If you made an incorrect selection, and their selection was correct, then you’ll hear a low tone followed by a high tone. Once the game is finished, you will be shown the final results.
